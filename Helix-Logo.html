Helix Logo Design

3 weeks of client meetings, ideation, and graphic design to create a logo for a new small business.
 Client: Jeni Healy
 July 2020
Skills
  • Graphic Design
  • Typography
Tools
  • Illustrator

Overview

Helix Global Strategies is a brand new consulting LLC for health and global health policy started by Jeni Healy. She asked me to design a logo and the beginnings of a brand to get her company off the ground.

Ideation

After meeting with the client, I began to brainstorm. Jeni wanted a classic, professional font and visual ties to the healthcare industry. I developed a range of low-fidelity concepts in Adobe Illustrator. You can see some of the icon and typography concepts below.

Refining the Idea

I worked with the client to choose three broad concepts and continued to iterate with different colors, textures, and shading. After another round of feedback with the client, I added the company name to the logo and began to test different fonts and text placement.

Final Logo

After several more iterations, we chose the final logo concept. I re-constructed the logo icon from scratch using a grid to ensure that the shipped logo was pixel-perfect. Finally, I created several logo variations for the client’s various branding needs.

Throughout the design process, I flexed my creativity and developed my branding skills — logo design is just one part of branding that I find fascinating.
