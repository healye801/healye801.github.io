Overview

I led a team of three designers to create a revamp proposal for the internal USCIS homepage. All USCIS agents use this page to keep track of their cases and upcoming tasks, but the old version had several significant usability issues. The homepage is a large, unresponsive table with tens to hundreds of rows of data, and too many columns to display on the page all at once. Our proposal tackles the thorny issue of responsive enterprise tables (no small task!), while recognizing the familiarity of the well-established workflow and development limitations prevent a full redesign. The proposed design will impact thousands of users at every step in their workflow. The design is undergoing developer reviews and usability studies in 2023.
