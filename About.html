About

My name is Emma Healy, and I'm a student at Northwestern University studying Communications, Design, and Marketing (but you can read more about that if you download my resume here). I design because creative problemsolving can make life better.

I'm originally from a town called Oconomowoc, Wisconsin, and I make the drive up from Evanston often to visit my family and our two dogs, Baxter and Buddy.  

Look, design is my passion, but everybody needs to put the pen down sometimes.  When the Chicago weather cooperates, you can find me hammocking in the parks lining Lake Michigan; during winter snowstorms you can bet that I'm somewhere drinking hot chocolate and maybe watercolor printing, watching "Say Yes to the Dress," or learning a new vegetarian recipe.
