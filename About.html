About

My name is Emma Healy, and I'm a Product Designer at Alpha Omega Integrations in New York City (you can read more about that if you download my resume here). I'm originally from a town called Oconomowoc, Wisconsin, and I often visit my family and our two dogs, Baxter and Buddy. I am excited to create intuitive, beautiful experiences that make people's lives better.
Look, design is my passion, but everybody needs to shut their laptop sometimes.  I take oil painting and drawing lessons at the New York Academy of Art Continuing Studies program. When the weather cooperates, you can find me hammocking in Riverside Park; otherwise you can bet I'm somewhere drinking hot chocolate and maybe painting, watching movies, or learning a new vegetarian recipe.
