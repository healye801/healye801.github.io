About

My name is Emma Healy, and I'm an Associate Experience Designer at Publicis Sapient in Chicago (you can read more about that if you download my resume here). I love design because creative problemsolving can make life better and more beautiful.

I'm originally from a town called Oconomowoc, Wisconsin, and I make the drive up from Gold Coast often to visit my family and our two dogs, Baxter and Buddy.  

Look, design is my passion, but everybody needs to shut their laptop sometimes.  I take oil painting and drawing lessons at the Palette & Chisel -- a historic art studio coincidentally located in my neighborhood. When the Chicago weather cooperates, you can find me hammocking in the parks lining Lake Michigan; during winter snowstorms you can bet that I'm somewhere drinking hot chocolate and maybe painting, watching movies, or learning a new vegetarian recipe.
